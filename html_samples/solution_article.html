MAKING COMPLEX REQUIREMENTS TRANSPARENT

PERFECT PLANNING FROM THE VERY BEGINNING

At a time of rapid technological and scientific change a number of new questions and challenges are posed
which must be taken into consideration during the planning stage of a hospital or laboratory.

How can new devices be flexibly integrated into an existing OR?
What devices and connections must be planned for in the operating room or laboratory?
How can the paths for patients, personnel and material be designed in the best possible way?
These examples show that variable and flexible concepts form the basis of the introduction
and implementation of complex solutions in hospital and laboratory construction.

The objectives and demands of the various parties involved, such as specialist planners, medical staff,
technical service staff and architects must be fully taken into account.

An essential element for successful project management is thus the integration of all those involved both internally
and externally. When doing this, the main focus must always be on the objective of ensuring the best possible
use of resources for medical and scientific operation.
